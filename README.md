# 📊 Churn & Revenue Risk Dashboard (Power BI)

This project presents an end-to-end Power BI dashboard designed to analyze customer churn risk, revenue at risk, and growth opportunities.

---

## 🔍 Overview

The dashboard provides a comprehensive view of:

- Customer distribution and segmentation  
- High-risk and high-opportunity users  
- Revenue at risk  
- Behavioral patterns across devices and channels  

---

## 📸 Dashboard Overview

![Overview](overview.png)

---

## 🔎 Deep Analysis

Detailed breakdowns across multiple dimensions:

- Churn risk by device  
- User distribution by segment  
- Revenue at risk by traffic source  
- Opportunity score analysis  
- Category-based performance  

![Deep Analysis](deep_analysis.png)

---

## 🚀 Action Plan

Based on the insights, a structured action plan was created:

- Improve mobile user experience  
- Optimize organic traffic funnel  
- Target high-risk users with personalized campaigns  
- Focus on high-potential categories  

![Action Plan](action_plan.png)

---

## 💡 Key Insights

- Mobile users have higher churn risk  
- Organic Search contributes most to revenue at risk  
- Electronics category shows highest potential  
- High-risk users are concentrated in specific segments  

---

## 🎯 Business Goal

- Reduce churn rate  
- Decrease revenue at risk  
- Increase conversion rate  
- Improve overall customer value
